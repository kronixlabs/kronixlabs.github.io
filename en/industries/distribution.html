The Problem in Distribution

Order explosion without team scaling

Your sales are growing, but your operational team is drowning. Each new order adds 15-20 manual steps. You need someone managing orders 24/7.

Inventory blindness across multiple locations

You have stock scattered across warehouses, stores, and points of sale. No real-time visibility. Overselling one location while another is overstocked.

Customer service bottleneck

WhatsApp, email, calls, website chat — all demanding responses simultaneously. Your team can't answer in time. Customers get frustrated.

Manual payment reconciliation

Payments arrive through multiple channels. Bank transfers, cards, checks. Your team spends hours matching payments to orders and reconciling accounts.

Sales team buried in operations

Your best salespeople spend 40% of their time solving operational issues instead of selling. Quota is a dream, not a target.

Delivery coordination chaos

Managing delivery routes, driver communication, real-time tracking, and customer notifications manually. One dispatch error cascades into 20 problems.
