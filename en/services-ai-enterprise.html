Solution 02

Smart Energy

AI-powered energy metering & billing

System for measuring, managing and billing electricity with smart breakers and AI — designed for commercial space owners, business centers and industrial parks.

Each unit or commercial space has a smart breaker that measures real-time consumption, generates automatic reports and enables fair, transparent energy billing to each tenant. The AI detects abnormal usage, predicts demand peaks and can manage remote cut-off and reconnection.
