Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

We start with a Strategic Operational Audit (investment varies by company size). Once we identify impact points and opportunity areas, we design a proposal with projected ROI and timeline. No commitments before the audit. Transparency first.

Do you work with our team or replace them?

We work with your team. We enhance it with infrastructure. We don't replace people — we redesign how they work. Your team becomes more productive, not redundant. This is about leverage, not elimination.

What happens to my data during the process?

Your data remains under your control at all times. We sign NDA before any access. Our configurations are on-premise or hybrid according to your policy. Data sovereignty is non-negotiable in our framework.

Can you implement in a company that already uses automation tools?

Yes. We frequently optimize, consolidate, or replace existing automations within a coherent architecture. Most companies have point solutions that don't talk to each other. We integrate them or replace them strategically.
